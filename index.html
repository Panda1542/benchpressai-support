Important Safety Notice

BenchPress AI is a fitness tracking and training feedback tool. It is not a medical device, physical therapy device, injury prevention system, professional coaching replacement, or emergency safety system.

Bench pressing and weightlifting involve risk of serious injury, including muscle strains, joint injuries, dropped weights, crushing injuries, and other harm. Always use proper equipment, safety arms, collars where appropriate, and a qualified spotter when lifting heavy weight.

You are responsible for deciding whether you are physically able to exercise and whether your training setup is safe. Stop using the app and stop exercising if you feel pain, dizziness, shortness of breath, numbness, unusual discomfort, or any other concerning symptom. Consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning or changing an exercise program.

How to Use BenchPress AI

Place your iPhone behind your head on a stable tripod or secure mount with a clear view of the bench press movement. Start a set inside the app and allow the app to analyze bench press motion using the camera.

For best results, use good lighting, keep the phone stable, avoid blocking the camera view, and make sure the bench, bar, plates, and upper body are visible. Tracking accuracy can vary depending on lighting, camera angle, body position, clothing, equipment, and device performance.
