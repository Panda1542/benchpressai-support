How to Use BenchPress AI

Place your iPhone behind your head on a stable tripod, start a set, and allow the app to track bench press movement using the camera.

Always use proper lifting safety, a spotter, or safety arms when bench pressing.

Privacy

BenchPress AI uses the camera for workout tracking. Camera access is used to detect bench press movement and provide workout feedback.

We do not sell personal data.
